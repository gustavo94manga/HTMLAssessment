Description

One Piece is an ongoing Japanese comic book series (referred to as manga), it first debuted in 1997. Astonishingly enough, the show quickly picked up steam as it immediately sold millions of manga copies. It was an instant hit. 2 years later it aired on tv as an anime series. The series revolves around a certain pirate named Monkey D. Luffy, who is in search of the One Piece, a legendary treasure left behind by the Pirate King, who was assassinated by the World Governement. However, there are many challenges and ongoing mysteries that have spanned over 25 years to this day. Today, it is the best and most selling manga series of all time!
